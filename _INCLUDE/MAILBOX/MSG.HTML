Invitation to Apprenticeship Assessment Morning

Following your application for an Electrical Apprenticeship with Clarkson Evans, branch, I am delighted to confirm that you have been selected to attend an assessment morning at which will be held at our office ( click to see map ). You should aim to arrive 15 minutes early, ready to start at the time above. On arrival, please report to the Main Entrance.

Details

During the morning you will be asked to complete assessments covering Maths, English and practical skills. Finally, we will also carry out colour blindness screening. We anticipate finishing at approximately 12.15pm. If you have already completed your level 3 in Electrical Installations we will ask you to stay and complete a short theory test which will take an additional 30 minutes.

We suggest smart casual dress for the assessment day however as you will be carrying out a practical task, it is important that you are comfortable and wearing sturdy footwear. Free on-site parking is usually available, but you may need to be prepared to park on a roadside nearby at busy times. Please do not park in any spaces where signage indicates that they are allocated to neighbouring businesses.

Following the Assessment Morning

Final interviews will be organised within 2 weeks of the assessment morning.  We will notify you as soon as possible after the assessment morning whether you have been shortlisted to attend the final interview stage, which will consist of a more formal meeting with our Regional Head of Production and another Senior Manager.

ACTION REQUIRED NOW

To secure your place at our assessment morning, please email the following information to jobs@clarksonevans.co.uk  ASAP.

·         Date of birth.

·         Confirmation that you have a driving licence and can transport yourself to the branch and sites.

·         The type of licence you have – manual or automatic.

·         Confirmation that you have GCSE Maths and English at C or above, if not please advise the mark you received.

·         A recent ‘head and shoulders’ photograph of yourself, taken against a plain background (a selfie is fine!) to help us identify candidates on the day.

·         Details of any electrical qualifications you have. These may provide exemptions for parts of the apprenticeship.

Please note that we don’t require any certificates or scans of documents at this stage. If you are successful, we will ask you to provide these documents on your first day.

If you have any questions in the meantime or if you have any special requirements for the assessment morning, please do not hesitate to contact us.

We look forward to meeting you and wish you the very best of luck!
